# MERIDIAN — 3-Minute Demo Script

> Target: Live demo or screen recording for hackathon judges.
> Duration: 3 minutes (strict).
> Tone: Confident, clear, zero filler.

---

## [0:00 - 0:20] The Hook

**Speaker:**

> "Right now, there is $100 billion sitting in DeFi. And if you are a retail user, you are leaving almost half your yield on the table. Not because better opportunities do not exist -- but because finding them requires a quant team you do not have.
>
> Meridian fixes that. One deposit. Maximum yield. Let me show you."

*Screen: Meridian landing page. Clean. Minimal. The tagline is visible.*

---

## [0:20 - 0:50] Connect and Deposit

**Speaker:**

> "Here is the Meridian dashboard. I connect my wallet -- we support MetaMask, WalletConnect, anything EVM-compatible -- and I am in."

*Action: Click "Connect Wallet." Wallet popup appears. Approve connection.*

> "I see my portfolio overview. Right now it is empty. Let me fix that."

*Action: Navigate to the Vault page. Show the available vault (e.g., "USDC Yield Vault").*

> "I select the USDC vault. I enter my deposit amount -- let us say 1,000 USDC. One click. Done."

*Action: Enter 1000, click "Deposit." Show the transaction confirmation in the wallet. Transaction confirms on HashKey Chain.*

> "That is it. My capital is now live. Meridian takes it from here."

---

## [0:50 - 1:30] The AI Engine

**Speaker:**

> "Now here is where it gets interesting. Behind this vault sits our ML engine. Let me show you what it is actually doing."

*Action: Switch to the Strategy Dashboard or analytics panel showing the AI allocation breakdown.*

> "Meridian's AI is continuously scanning every yield opportunity on HashKey Chain. Lending rates, liquidity pools, staking rewards -- everything. It scores each one on risk-adjusted return using a model trained on historical DeFi data."

*Action: Point to the strategy allocation chart showing percentages across different protocols/strategies.*

> "Right now, the engine has allocated my capital across three strategies. 45% in a lending protocol, 35% in a liquidity pool, 20% in staking. Each scored, ranked, and selected automatically."

> "And this is not static. When conditions change -- a pool's APY drops, a new opportunity appears, risk increases somewhere -- the StrategyRouter contract rebalances. No action required from me."

---

## [1:30 - 2:10] Real-Time Performance

**Speaker:**

> "Let me show you the performance view."

*Action: Navigate to the portfolio performance panel. Show yield accrual, historical chart, current APY.*

> "I can see my current yield rate, my accumulated earnings, and a full history of every rebalance the AI has executed -- all indexed through The Graph and fully on-chain verifiable."

*Action: Point to the rebalance history log showing timestamps and allocation changes.*

> "Every decision the AI makes is recorded on-chain. This is not a black box. You can verify every move. Full transparency."

*Action: Click on a rebalance event. Show the on-chain transaction on HashKey Chain explorer.*

> "There it is. On-chain. Immutable. Auditable."

---

## [2:10 - 2:40] The Differentiator

**Speaker:**

> "Let me put this in perspective. A typical retail user manually farms one pool, maybe two. They check yields once a day if they are disciplined. They miss opportunities constantly."

> "Meridian's AI evaluates every available strategy, every epoch. It rebalances automatically. It manages risk you did not even know existed. And it does this for a 10% performance fee -- meaning we only earn when you earn."

> "No deposit fee. No withdrawal fee. Fully aligned incentives."

*Action: Show the fee structure on the vault detail page.*

---

## [2:40 - 3:00] The Close

**Speaker:**

> "We built Meridian on HashKey Chain for a reason. Low gas makes frequent rebalancing viable. EVM compatibility means battle-tested contracts. And HashKey's regulatory clarity means this is a product retail users can actually trust."

> "One deposit. Maximum yield. Institutional intelligence. That is Meridian."

*Action: Return to portfolio view showing the vault with active yield accrual. Hold for 3 seconds.*

> "Thank you."

## Demo Checklist

- [ ] Wallet connected to HashKey Chain testnet
- [ ] Testnet USDC in wallet (enough for demo deposit)
- [ ] Smart contracts deployed and verified
- [ ] Subgraph synced and serving data
- [ ] ML engine running and producing allocation signals
- [ ] All transactions pre-tested to ensure they confirm quickly
- [ ] Screen recording backup ready (in case live demo fails)
- [ ] Browser clean -- no other tabs, no notifications, no clutter

## Fallback Plan

If live transactions are slow or fail during demo:
1. Switch to pre-recorded screen capture of the same flow
2. Narrate live over the recording
3. Never acknowledge the switch -- keep energy high
