# MERIDIAN — Video Storyboard

> Format: 60-90 second submission video
> Style: Cinematic product reveal. Clean motion graphics. Voiceover-driven.
> Music: Electronic ambient -- builds tension, resolves into clarity.
> Resolution: 1920x1080 minimum. 4K preferred.

---

## Act 1: The Problem (0:00 - 0:20)

### Scene 1 — The Chaos (0:00 - 0:10)

**Visual:** Fast montage. Multiple browser tabs. DeFi dashboards everywhere. Numbers flying. Red and green candles. A user scrolling frantically through yield farms. Overwhelming. Chaotic. Human.

**Motion:** Quick cuts, slight camera shake, screen glare effects.

**Voiceover:**
> "DeFi has $100 billion in liquidity. Hundreds of protocols. Thousands of yield opportunities."

**Text overlay (final frame of montage):**
> `AND RETAIL USERS CAPTURE LESS THAN HALF.`

### Scene 2 — The Gap (0:10 - 0:20)

**Visual:** Split screen. Left side: a solo user staring at a laptop, confused. Right side: a hedge fund trading floor, multiple monitors, algorithms running. The contrast is stark.

**Motion:** Slow zoom out. Both sides visible simultaneously.

**Voiceover:**
> "Institutions run quantitative models to find alpha. Everyone else... guesses. That gap is not a feature. It is a failure."

**Text overlay:**
> `40-60% OF YIELD. LEFT ON THE TABLE.`

**Voiceover:**

**Voiceover:**

## Production Notes

### Voiceover
- Tone: Calm authority. Steve Jobs presenting the iPhone, not a used car salesman.
- Pace: Measured. Let the words land. Silence between sections is powerful.
- Voice: Deep, clear, male or female -- either works if delivery is confident and unhurried.

### Music
- Reference tracks: Hans Zimmer "Time" (energy curve), Trent Reznor "Social Network" soundtrack (texture)
- Starts low and ambient in Act 1, builds through Act 2, peaks at the numbers in Act 3, resolves cleanly in Act 4.
- No lyrics. No distracting melodies. Texture and rhythm only.

### Typography
- Font: Inter or SF Pro Display. Clean. Modern. No serifs.
- Weight: Bold for headlines, Regular for body.
- Color: White text on dark backgrounds. Accent color from Meridian brand palette.

### Color Palette
- Primary background: Deep navy / near-black (#0A0E1A)
- Accent: Electric teal or gold (brand color)
- Text: White (#FFFFFF) and muted gray (#A0A0B0)
- Charts/data: Teal highlights with subtle gradients
